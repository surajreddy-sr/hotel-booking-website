Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking by logging into your account on our website or mobile app, navigating to "My Bookings," selecting the reservation you wish to cancel, and following the cancellation prompts. Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team for assistance.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation. However, it may take additional time for the refund to appear on your credit card statement, depending on your bank's processing times.

Can I modify my booking instead of canceling?

Yes, in most cases you can modify your booking (change dates, room type, etc.) without canceling. Modifications may be subject to rate differences and availability. There's usually no fee for modifications made outside the cancellation window.

What if I need to cancel due to an emergency?

We understand that emergencies happen. In cases of documented emergencies (medical, death in family, etc.), we may offer more flexible cancellation options. Please contact our customer service team with supporting documentation as soon as possible.

Are there any non-refundable booking options?

Yes, we sometimes offer discounted non-refundable rates. These will be clearly marked during the booking process and in your confirmation email. Non-refundable bookings cannot be canceled or changed for any reason.
